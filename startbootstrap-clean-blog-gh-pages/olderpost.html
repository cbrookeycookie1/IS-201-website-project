I am a member of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints

My religion

I am a member of the church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints. I was born an raised in this church and I am glad I was. Since I can remember, I've gone to church with my parents and they helped me have faith in Jesus Christ.

When I turned 19 I served a mission. I went to the California Arcadia Mission speaking Mandarin Chinese from Jun 2020-Nov 2021, so it was during COVID. Serving a mission during this pandemic was hard but it was well worth it because we still saw many miracles.

"Behold, are not the things that God hath wrought marvelous in our eyes? Yea, and who can comprehend the marvelous works of God?" -Mormon 9:16

One of the things I value most is education. I have come to realize that there are so many things we can learn every single day. However, we will not learn anything unless we try and put effort into it.

Afterwards

After coming home from my mission I got accepted into BYU where I am continuing to strengthen my faith in Christ through attending religion classes, going to church, and participating in many of the churhc activities.

I recommend all to serve a full-time mission because it changed my life and I know it can change yours.
